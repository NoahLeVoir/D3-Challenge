What does this data tell us??

As we can see, the lack of health care has a positive correlation with the percentage of a states people experiencing poverty. The same can be said of the correlation between a higher percentage who smoke and a the percentage of people experiencing poverty. Unfortunately, we see that states like Mississippi and New Mexico rank towards the high end in all three of those categories. It can also be seen that the average age of a states population is not a great determinator of either health factors on the y axis as we see the bubbles huddle pretty close together!
